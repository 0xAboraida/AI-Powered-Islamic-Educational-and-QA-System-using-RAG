### 1. The Curation Philosophy

The selection of these books was **not random**. Every single book integrated into the Zad-AI platform was manually chosen because it is considered a foundational pillar ("Umm al-Kutub") in its respective Islamic science.

Furthermore, for many of these classical texts, there are dozens of digital editions available online. We did not simply download the first one we found. We meticulously evaluated multiple versions of the same book and selected the absolute **best edition** based on:
1. **Structural Formatting:** Proper hierarchical layout (Chapters, Sections, Sub-sections) to aid the chunking algorithms.
2. **Footnotes & Citations:** Clean separation of the main text from the author's footnotes to prevent hallucination during retrieval.
3. **Diacritics (Tashkeel):** Extremely high accuracy in Arabic diacritics to ensure the Embeddings model and LLM interpret the semantic meaning flawlessly without ambiguity.
4. **Authenticity:** Editions verified by trusted Islamic research centers.
